DL&SC work has direct, named action-level alignment to Pillars 1, 2, 5, and 6 of the NC 2025–2030 Strategic Plan Achieving Educational Excellence. This section shows where our DECC contributions fulfill explicit state strategic obligations.

P1

Prepare Each Student for Their Next Phase in Life

P1.F2.A2 · Launch February 2026
Strengthen digital learning and literacy PreK–12
Develop high-quality, standards-aligned online content; formalize NCDPI's approach to supporting PSUs with a data-driven framework for effective digital and personalized learning; continue to guide responsible AI use.
DL&SC programs advancing this action:
Digital Learning Plan (DLI) AI 2.0 Roadmap INTERNAL Digital Learning Standards AI Literacy for Teachers Course AI Resources Hub
P1.F4.A5 · Launch August 2026
Embed AI safely, responsibly, and ethically in learning
Establish scalable approaches to embed artificial intelligence safely, responsibly and ethically, and ensure collaborative problem-solving is included in the learning experience.
DL&SC programs advancing this action:
NCDPI GenAI Use Policy INTERNAL AI Literacy for Teachers Course AI Solve-A-Thon AI-Assisted Micro PBL 2.0
P2

Revere Public School Educators

P2.F1.A3 · Launch August 2026
Statewide standards for high-quality professional learning, including microcredentials
Develop, adopt and promote statewide standards for high-quality professional learning to serve as guidance for the design and evaluation of PD opportunities, including microcredentials.
DL&SC programs advancing this action:
Micro-Credentials Program NCBOLD Summer Professional Learning AI Internal Training Program INTERNAL Professional Learning Hub
P5

Optimize Operational Excellence

P5.F3.A1 · Launch August 2025
Implement NCSIS (Infinite Campus) statewide
Full statewide implementation of the NC Student Information System with minimal critical incidents in the first year. Direct operational responsibility of the DL&SC office.
DL&SC programs advancing this action:
NCSIS (Infinite Campus) NCEd Connect Platform NCEd Connect Symposium
P5.F3.A6 · Launch January 2027
Leverage AI and advanced analytics to enhance NCDPI efficiency
Explore ways to leverage machine learning, AI and advanced analytics to enhance NCDPI efficiency and extend value and insights essential to all statewide stakeholders.
DL&SC programs advancing this action:
AI Governance INTERNAL AI Project Development with NC State INTERNAL AI 2.0 Roadmap INTERNAL
P6

Lead Transformative Change

P6.F2.A1 · Launch September 2026
Statewide, regional, and partner education networks
Establish statewide, regional and partner education networks to facilitate implementation of key strategic plan priorities, share scalable resources and support continuous improvement.
DL&SC programs advancing this action:
Digital Leaders Exchange NCBOLD Summer Professional Learning DECC / DESC Participation
P6.F2.A3 · Launch October 2027
Annual statewide and interstate networks conference
Convene an annual statewide and interstate networks conference to showcase innovative PreK–12 practices, host immersive site visits and expand access to research-driven models.

DECC / DESC Participation (CCSSO) Interstate AI Collaborative / SETDA AI Together / Ballmer Grant Year Two AGI/ASI Scenarios / Ballmer Grant Year Two
